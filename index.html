Different Caloric Intakes

The following will display the differences that occur during different caloric intakes.

Deficit

Surplus

Maintenance

During a deficit, the goal is often to loose weight while retaining as much muscle mass as possible. To achieve that goal, a 500-1000 daily caloric deficit needs to occur while maintaining adequate protien and fat levels. Protein should be equivilent to your bodyweight in grams while fat should be 30% of your daily caloric intake.
During a surplus, the goal is to gain weight while also attempting to gain the most amount of muscle possible. I personally recommending intaking a surplus of only 100-250 calories daily to prevent as much fat gain as possible. Protein levels should exceed your bodyweight by 20-40 grams for bodybuilding/ powerlifting purposes.
